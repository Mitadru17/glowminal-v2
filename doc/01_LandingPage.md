# LandingPage.md

> This specification defines the complete experience for the Glowminal
> marketing website and entry point into the application.

# Objective

Convert first-time visitors into registered users by building trust
before asking them to upload a photo.

Success is measured by:

-   Visitors understand what Glowminal does within 10 seconds.
-   Users trust the platform enough to start a free scan.
-   Users understand that Glowminal is **not** a replacement for a
    dermatologist.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Target Audience

Primary: - People 18--35 interested in skincare. - Beginners who don't
know where to start. - Existing skincare users wanting objective
analysis.

# Glowminal --- Brand Guidelines

> **Brand Promise:** Make understanding your skin feel as trustworthy,
> calm, and intuitive as checking your health.

# 1. Brand Identity

## Positioning

Glowminal is **not** a beauty app. Glowminal is **not** a cosmetics
marketplace.

Glowminal is a **Skin Intelligence Platform** that uses AI to help users
understand, monitor, and improve their skin over time.

## Mission

Democratize science-backed skincare through explainable artificial
intelligence.

## Vision

Become the world's most trusted platform for long-term skin
intelligence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Core Brand Values

-   Trust before trends
-   Science over marketing
-   Simplicity over complexity
-   Long-term progress over quick fixes
-   Privacy by default
-   Explainable AI
-   Calm experiences

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Brand Personality

Glowminal should feel:

-   Intelligent
-   Calm
-   Premium
-   Human
-   Honest
-   Reassuring
-   Modern

Avoid sounding: - Salesy - Clinical - Fear-driven - Overly playful -
Trend-chasing

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Tone of Voice

## Writing Style

Short, clear, conversational.

Instead of: "You exhibit significant facial erythema."

Use: "We detected noticeable redness around your cheeks."

Never exaggerate certainty. Always communicate confidence levels.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Visual Philosophy

Design keywords:

-   Minimal
-   Spacious
-   Organic
-   Scientific
-   Elegant
-   Premium

Every screen should reduce anxiety, not increase it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Color Philosophy

Primary Brand Green: #3CA956

Supporting Green: #2F8143

Background: #F5F7F4

Surface: #FFFFFF

Text Primary: #222722

Text Secondary: #5B6A5C

Divider: #E2E8E1

Error: #D64545

Use generous whitespace. Avoid loud gradients and neon effects.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Typography

Preferred: Inter

Fallback: Geist Roboto

Hierarchy:

-   H1
-   H2
-   H3
-   Body
-   Caption

Readable first. Decorative never.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Photography

Images should use:

-   Natural lighting
-   Neutral backgrounds
-   Real skin textures
-   Diverse skin tones
-   Minimal editing

Avoid unrealistic beauty filters.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Iconography

Use simple rounded line icons.

Consistent stroke width.

Minimal detail.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Illustrations

Use soft organic shapes.

Medical illustrations should remain clean and educational.

Avoid cartoonish artwork.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Motion

Animations should communicate state changes.

Recommended duration:

180--250 ms.

Examples:

-   Fade
-   Scale
-   Slide

Avoid flashy transitions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 12. UX Principles

Every feature should answer:

1.  What happened?
2.  Why did it happen?
3.  What should I do next?

Never leave users confused.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 13. Trust Principles

Every AI insight must include:

-   Confidence score
-   Explanation
-   Suggested next action

Never imply medical diagnosis.

Always include an appropriate disclaimer where necessary.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 14. Accessibility

Meet WCAG AA.

High contrast.

Keyboard navigation.

Screen reader support.

Touch targets \>=44px.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 15. Brand Do's

-   Educate users
-   Encourage healthy routines
-   Celebrate progress
-   Use evidence-based language
-   Be transparent

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 16. Brand Don'ts

-   Shame users
-   Promise miracle results
-   Overuse technical jargon
-   Push unnecessary products
-   Hide uncertainty

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 17. Future Brand Evolution

Phase 1: Skin Intelligence

Phase 2: AI Skin Coach

Phase 3: Personal Skin Operating System

Long-term ambition:

Glowminal becomes the default place people go to understand the story of
their skin over months and years.
